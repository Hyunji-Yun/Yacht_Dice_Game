Yacht Dice Game (also known as Yatzy, Generala, or Cheerio) is a turn-based dice game where players aim to score the highest total by rolling combinations of five dice.

Basic Gameplay:

  • Setup: Each player uses a scorecard with various scoring categories.
  • Turns: Players take turns rolling dice up to three times per turn.
  • Rolling: Keep desired dice and re-roll the rest.
  • Scoring: After rolling, choose a category and record the score (can be zero).
  • Winning: When all categories are filled, the player with the highest total wins.

  • Lower Section: 3 of a Kind, 4 of a Kind, Full House, Small/Large Straight, Choice, Yacht.
  • Bonus: 35 points if Upper Section scores reach 63+.

Example Scoring:

  • Full House: 25 points for a pair + three of a kind.
  • Small Straight: 30 points for 4-sequence (e.g., 1-2-3-4).
  • Large Straight: 40 points for 5-sequence (e.g., 1-2-3-4-5).
  • Yacht: 50 points if all five dice are the same.
  • Choice: Sum of all dice.
